SpaceX, Tesla and Paypal Founder

  • Elon Musk was born to engineer father and model mother on June 28, 1971.

  • At 8 years old, he ran out of books to read at the library so he read the entire Encyclopedia Britannica.

  • Elon learned BASIC by finishing a 6 month book in 3 days.

  • At age 12, writes video game Blastar and sells it for $500.

  • Starts college at age 18 in Ontario.

  • Cold calls execs he reads about in the newspaper, asking to meet for lunch.

  • Top exec of bank responds and gives him an internship.

  • Sells computers out of his dorm room.

  • Transfers to UPenn double majoring in physics and economics.

  • Starts Zip2 with brother, coding it himself. Later gets 22 million from sale of Zip2.

  • Starts X.com, an online bank, with his own money in 1999.

  • Merges with Confinity a year later forming PayPal

  • Learns how to build rockets from books and then starts SpaceX in June, 2002.

  • In 2004, meets Marc and Martin, Tesla founders, and suggests they work with JB Straubel on electric cars.

  • After three unsuccessful rocket lauches, SpaceX and Tesla start running out of cash.

  • In September of 2008, fourth rocket lauch succeeds and Tesla raises funding before going bankrupt.

  • In 2011, he promises to send humans to Mars by 2021-2031.

  • Announces reusable rockets making space flight 100 times cheaper, first one successfully lands in 2015.

  • Model 3 production is on track to start mid 2017.

  • By 2018, the Gigafactory will reach full capacity.

  • By 2025, Mars Colonial Transporter launches.

  • Tickets to Mars will be 500,000 within Musk's lifetime.
